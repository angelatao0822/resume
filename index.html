Biography

i was born on 22nd aug 1994 in beijing, capital of china. i am currently studying bachelor of visual communication in western sydney university. i am quite passionate about graphic design particular in digital art design. influenced by multicultural elements, i have gained a great talent in both hand drafting and computer skills and created my own artworks. in addition, i have been developing relative skills as well such as web design, photo-media and other forms of graphic designs. always work hard, never stop to learn is my study attitude. “Simplicity, creativity, honesty”is my brand value.

i enjoy sketching and other kinds of art. since i was young, i found i had a distinct gift for drawing and always joined art activities to further develop my drawing skills and perception of beauty. when i got in touch with multiculture, such as japanese animation or american hero movie, i learnt to create complex digital art using illustrator and photoshop. now i’m able to make artwork skilfully. also, i do well in reading and writing.

in addition, i am interested in a variety of sports as well. i’m a regular gym-goer doing exercise four to five times a week, and always join sports activities or outdoor challenges to strength my body and confidence.
